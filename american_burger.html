American Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Lettuce leaf
  • 1 tomato slice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, and form a patty.
  2. Cook the patty on a grill or pan for about 4-5 minutes on each side.
  3. Add a slice of cheese on top of the patty to melt.
  4. Toast the hamburger bun until golden brown.
  5. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, the cooked patty, and condiments of your choice.
  6. Serve with fries or your favorite sides.
