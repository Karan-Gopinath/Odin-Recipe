Indian Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or mixed vegetables
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, and cook until soft, creating a thick base.
  4. Add spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander) and stir well.
  5. Add chicken or vegetables, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook until fully done.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
