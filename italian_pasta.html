Italian Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (your choice of type)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat some olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream to the pan and simmer for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until well-coated.
  6. Serve hot with extra Parmesan cheese on top if desired.
