Japanese Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 100g salmon or tuna (optional)
  • 4 sheets of nori (seaweed)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Drain.
  2. Cook the rice with water in a rice cooker. Once done, let it cool slightly.
  3. Mix rice vinegar into the rice for flavor.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat. Spread rice evenly on the nori.
  5. Add cucumber, avocado, and salmon slices on top of the rice.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly with the mat, then slice it into pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
