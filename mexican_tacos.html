Mexican Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 small tortillas
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup guacamole
  • Salt, pepper, and taco seasoning to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, cook ground beef with salt, pepper, and taco seasoning until browned.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or in the microwave.
  3. Assemble tacos by filling each tortilla with beef, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and guacamole.
  4. Serve immediately with lime wedges if desired.
